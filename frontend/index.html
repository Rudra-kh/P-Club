About Our Club

We are a passionate community of photographers at IIIT Naya Raipur, dedicated to exploring the art of visual storytelling. Whether you're a beginner with a smartphone or an experienced photographer with professional gear, you'll find your place here.

Our club organizes weekly workshops, photo walks, competitions, and exhibitions throughout the year. Join us every Thursday at 6 PM in the Student Center, Room 204.
